## Operating Frequencies

### LoRa® Frequency

Long Range LoRaWAN® operating in the 433 MHz, 470 MHz, 863 MHz, or 928 MHz frequency bands.

| Certification | Frequency Range   |
| ------------- | ----------------- |
| FCC           | 902 MHz - 928 MHz |
| CE            | 863 MHz - 870 MHz |
| MIC           | 920 MHz - 928 MHz |
| KCC           | 920 MHz - 923 MHz |

### Bluetooth BLE

The bluetooth BLE operates at frequency of 2.4GHz.
